Rumble in Romblon: Hontiveros Calls for Senate Probe on Mining Scandal of Win Gatchalian-Linked Firm. AlamMoBa.net 06-Feb-2023

Senator Risa Hontiveros has called on the Senate to investigate the alleged illegal operations of Altai Philippines Mining Corporation (APMC) in Sibuyan Island, Romblon, amid strong opposition from residents and environmental groups due to the threats it poses to the area’s biodiversity.

Hontiveros filed Senate Resolution 459 days after a violent altercation happened in Sibuyan Island between the police and residents, who had formed a barricade to stop Altai Mining from entering the area. At least two persons were reportedly injured in the incident.

“Hindi dapat nauuwi sa karahasan ang mapayapang pagtutol ng mga residente sa panghihimasok ng mga kumpanyang nagmimina sa sarili nilang tahanan. Gusto lamang protektahan ng mga residente ang likas-yaman sa kanilang lugar. The residents are well within their rights to protest,” Hontiveros said.

-Politic.PH
