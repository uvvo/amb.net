Thousands participate in the "walk of faith" before the Nazarene feast. AlamMoBa.net 05-Jan-2023

MANILA, Philippines – On Sunday, January 8, thousands of followers participated in a "walk of faith" to remember the Feast of the Black Nazarene, which used to draw millions of people each year but hasn't fully recovered since the COVID-19 outbreak. The walk, which began at the Quirino Grandstand around 1 a.m. on Sunday and finished at the Quiapo Church at 4 a.m., drew about 88,000 enthusiasts.

The Transaction, an annual gathering of devotees in Manila for the relocation of the Black Nazarene from Luneta to Quip, will be replaced with the "walk of faith," the Quiapo church announced previously.

Families and organizations that attended the event were spotted carrying candles, flags, and pictures of the Black Nazarene. Others lifted towels that are often used to clean a statue of the Black Nazarene.

Ginebra is one victory away from winning the Commissioner's Cup. AlamMoBa.net 08-Jan-2023

JUST IN: After fending off a comeback attempt by the Bay Area Dragons to win Game 5 of the finals 101-91 on Sunday at the Mall of Asia Arena in Pasay City, Barangay Ginebra came one step closer to capturing the PBA Commissioner's Cup.

In front of 21,823 ecstatic spectators, Stanley Pringle made two three-pointers during a crucial period of the fourth quarter to help Ginebra take a 3-2 series lead. He had 20 points and 5 rebounds when the game was over.

For Bay Area, Game 4 hero Kobey Lam scored 16 points and grabbed three rebounds, while Hayden Blankley exploded for 29 points, nine rebounds, and three assists. On January 11 at the Smart Araneta Coliseum in Quezon City, Ginebra can finish the series.

Anak ni DOJ Sec. Remulla abswelto ng korte sa kasong illegal drug possession. AlamMoBa.net 08-Jan-2023

Inabswelto ng Las Piñas City Regional Trial Court ang anak ni Justice Secretary Jesus Crispin “Boying” Remulla sa kasong illegal drug possession.

Ayon kay Atty. Pearlito Campanilla, abogado ni Juanito Jose Remulla III, pinawalang sala ang kanyang kliyente dahil napatunayang hindi ito ang importer ng kush na nagkakahalaga ng P1.3-million. Dagdag pa ni Campanilla nagkaroon din umano ng isyu sa chain of custody sa mga ebidensiya dahil hindi nagpatawag ng mga witness ang Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency para sa inventory ng iligal na droga. Binigyan diin din ng abogado na ang package na naglalaman ng iligal na droga ay nakapangalan kay “Juanito Remulla,” at hindi kay Juanito Jose Remulla III.
