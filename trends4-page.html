Donnalyn Bartolome receives criticism for her "toxic positivity" after making a comment about being thankful for work after the holidays. AlamMoBa.net 03-Jan-2023

After telling her followers to simply be "grateful" for having a job despite returning to work after the Christmas season, Donnalyn Bartolome has once again drawn the wrath of internet users.

“Bakit may sad dahil back to work na? Diba dapat masaya ka kasi may chance ka na pagandahin buhay mo at ng pamilya? Trip ko pa nga may work ng January 1 dahil superstition ko may work ako buong taon pag ganun. Dapat grateful kasi may work,” she said

The vlogger further stated that her claim was only a "reminder" to her social media fans. "I hope you discover work that makes you happy if your job doesn't. This is only a reminder that having a job is a blessing, she continued, "bessss shift thinking, it's 2023. Yung pakikiligin ka and sheet [emoji]. This is just a reminder that having a job is a blessing. Bes, alter your perspective.

It's 2023. (If work makes you sad, I hope you find a job that makes you happy.) Netizens were drawn to Bartolome's message, especially on Facebook and Twitter, where several criticized her for being "rich" and "out of touch with the hardship of average Filipinos."
